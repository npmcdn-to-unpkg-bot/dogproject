About the bread

The Beautiful Puppy is an ACHC recognized Hybrid Dog Breed. A cross between the English Bulldog and the Beagle, it combines two great canine temperaments. This breed should be of a short and stocky build, and weigh between 30-50 pounds in most cases. On the English Bulldog side of the breed, you could expect a loyal and loving family companion. On the Beagle side, you are getting one of the best hunting/tracking hounds known to man. The combination of these two breeds should produce an excellent family pet and playful companion. While this is a well like mixed breed, it is not currently recognized as a purebred by the AKC or ACA.
